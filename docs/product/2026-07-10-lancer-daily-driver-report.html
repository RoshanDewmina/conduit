03 · Why Lancer — the reason to choose us

Omnara, Orca, and Happier are remote chat windows — built for watching your agent. Lancer is built for the time you're not watching. Chat is copyable in a day; a governed unattended loop is not.

“Don't watch your agents — govern them.”

The wedge has two halves that are load-bearing on each other: hands-free is only safe because governance sits under it · governance is only felt through hands-free.

The substance — governance (built, none of them have it)

  • Policy engine with risk tiers, fail-closed defaults
  • Content-hash-verified approvals — kills the approve-what-changed race
  • Hash-chained audit trail · atomic emergency stop
  • Proof receipts (lancer.proof/v0) with honest per-vendor confidence
  • Multi-vendor on your machines, your subscriptions — incl. OpenCode

The surface — deepest iOS-native layer (core identity)

  • Lock-screen approve/deny with the phone in your pocket
  • Live Activities as the glanceable away-state
  • Siri / App Intents: status, deny, pause, stop, voice-answer — 26-safe slice already merged
  • iOS 27 deep layer: Siri-dispatched runs, semantic search, on-device copilot — Apple-gated to ~Sept 14 GA
  • Voice-approve: never — the trust line that makes the rest credible
Standing caveat: this thesis must survive the dated competitive re-check — all three competitors are open-source and moving (Omnara already ships a Watch app). Verify the gap before doubling down publicly.
