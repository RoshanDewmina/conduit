What Omnara / Happy actually are

Mobile notification relays. An agent runs, needs you, sends a push, you tap approve. That's the whole loop. No policy, no rules, no "runs without you" concept. You are always the bottleneck.

What Conduit could be (the actual bet)

Your agents run unattended under rules you set. The policy handles 90% of decisions automatically. You only get interrupted for genuinely risky or ambiguous moments. You're in control without being in the loop.

The honest question

Does anyone actually want to run agents unattended — or does everyone still want to watch every step? That's what we need to answer before building more. The policy engine is the bet. If it's right, Conduit wins. If people want to approve everything, we're just a slower Omnara.
